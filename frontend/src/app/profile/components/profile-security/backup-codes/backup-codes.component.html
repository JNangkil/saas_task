How to Use Backup Codes
When to Use Backup Codes

Use your backup codes when you can't access your authenticator app:

  • You've lost your phone or can't install your authenticator app
  • Your authenticator app is not working properly
  • You're traveling and don't have access to your authenticator
  • Your device's battery is dead and you can't open your authenticator
Important Security Notes
  • Each backup code can only be used once
  • Store your backup codes in a secure location
  • Don't share your backup codes with anyone
  • Regenerate new codes if you suspect any have been compromised
